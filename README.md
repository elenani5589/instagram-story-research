## 🛡️ Privacy Features

This tool provides several layers of protection for your social media usage. These features run in the background while you navigate the platform.

### View stories without notice
When you view a story, the platform usually sends a signal to the owner. This extension blocks that signal. You view stories as often as you want without appearing on the viewer list. The owner never sees your profile in their analytics.

### Hide read receipts
Reading a message normally triggers a "Seen" status for the sender. This tool intercepts that communication. You read messages at your own pace without notifying the sender. You keep your availability private.

### Disable typing indicators
Typing a response often reveals your activity to the recipient. This software suppresses those indicators. You write your replies in private. The other person sees no sign of your activity while you compose your text.

### Manage online presence
Social media platforms show your friends when you are active. This extension masks your status. You appear offline even while you browse stories or scroll through your feed.
